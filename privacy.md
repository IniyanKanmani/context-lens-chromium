# Privacy Policy for Context Lens Extension

**Last Updated: October 25, 2025**

 Context Lens ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

- **Highlighted Text**: We collect the text you select on web pages for AI processing.
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, or IP addresses.

## How We Use Your Information

- To generate AI-powered contextual explanations for the highlighted text.
- Data is sent to [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai) for processing and is not stored by us.

## Data Sharing and Security

- We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties.
- Data is processed by OpenRouter, which adheres to Zero Data Retention (ZDR) policies for selected providers.
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect data in transit.

## Third-Party Services

This extension integrates with OpenRouter. Their privacy practices are governed by their [Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy). We encourage you to review it.

## Your Rights

- You can stop using the extension at any time.
- For data deletion requests related to OpenRouter, contact them directly.
